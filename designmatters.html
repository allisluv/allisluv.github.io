Why Hair Dye?

Clothing has many purposes including providing protection and modesty, designating gender or status, creating a group identity, and protecting the wearer. But the primary purpose of clothing is decoration and expression. Historians have decided this based on the fact that before clothing existed, humans had been expressing themselves thought tattoos, body piercings, and hair color.

"Aesthetic tendencies drift with the cultural currents, but our drive to alter ourselves is constant."

People have been going to extreme measures to dye their hair for millennia. Some techniques are gross and some are dangerous. Different times and different cultures value different hair qualities and colors. Like clothing and tattoos, people keep doing it throughout time and in all places. The question now is what we will come up with next!
