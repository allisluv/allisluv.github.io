High Contrast Portrait - Fall 2014

This was done in Photoshop as an attempt to turn a photo of an artist into a high contrast portrait with a distinct color scheme and without loosing the essence of the artist.

Acheivements - I chose to use a portrait of Amy Whinehouse. The original image is represented by the black. The rest of the color scheme works very well because the high contrast in the green and red. I think the red in the lips stands out and gives the image a little of Amy Winehouseâ€™s eccentricities.

Challenges - The darker green stripes in the background were added to fill empty space. I had to decide how best to fill the space. I played with shape and color, but it seemed best to keep it simple: straight lines and and a darker shade of the same green as the background.
